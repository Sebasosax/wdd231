Salt Lake Diabetes Community

Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Salt Lake Diabetes Community

This site name was selected because it clearly represents a local community effort focused on supporting people living with diabetes in the Salt Lake area. It reflects collaboration between clinics, nutritionists, and non-profit organizations.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide educational information about diabetes and to help individuals find local clinics, nutrition professionals, and non-profit organizations that offer medical care, education, and financial assistance.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find reliable information about diabetes?
  • Where can I get financial assistance for insulin or other diabetes-related medications?
  • Which clinics or organizations can help me based on my personal situation?

Color Scheme

  • #D12300: Used for the header and footer to create a strong visual identity.
  • #000000: Used for body text to ensure readability.
  • #FFFFFF / #F5F5F5: Used for backgrounds to improve contrast and accessibility.

Typography

  • Outfit: Used for headings and titles.
  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs.
